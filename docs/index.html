Problem 2: From 3D global coordinates to 2D image coordinates

(Ground Truth / Training set)

    

Camera Projective Geometry

Projection from world coordinates to image coordinates:

$O_{image} = K[R|t] O_{world}$


$O_{image}^{'} = M^{'}K[R|t] O_{world}$

$O_{image}^{'} = KM^{'}[R|t] O_{world}$


where $M^{'} = \begin{bmatrix} 1 & 0 & x_{v}\\ 0 & 1 & y_{v}\\ 0 & 0 & 1\\ \end{bmatrix} \begin{bmatrix} cos(\pi) & -sin(\pi) & 0\\ sin(\pi) & cos(\pi) & 0\\ 0 & 0 & 1\\ \end{bmatrix} \begin{bmatrix} 1 & 0 & -x_{v}\\ 0 & 1 & -y_{v}\\ 0 & 0 & 1\\ \end{bmatrix} = \begin{bmatrix} -1 & 0 & 2x_{v}\\ 0 & -1 & 2y_{v}\\ 0 & 0 & 1\\ \end{bmatrix}$

where $M^{'} = \begin{bmatrix} cos(\pi) & -sin(\pi) & 0\\ sin(\pi) & cos(\pi) & 0\\ 0 & 0 & 1\\ \end{bmatrix} = \begin{bmatrix} -1 & 0 & 0\\ 0 & -1 & 0\\ 0 & 0 & 1\\ \end{bmatrix}$


For a camera at (x, y, z) that produces a (w, h) camera image:

  • Intrinsic Matrix: $K = \begin{bmatrix} f & 0 & \frac{w}{2}\\ 0 & f & \frac{h}{2} \\ 0 & 0 & 1 \end{bmatrix} $ where $f = \frac{w}{2.0 * tan(fov\ *\frac{\pi}{360})}$

  • Extrinsic Matrix: $[R | t] = \begin{bmatrix} cos(k) & -sin(k) & 0 & x\\ sin(k) & cos(k) & 0 & y\\ 0 & 0 & 1 & z \\ 0 & 0 & 0 & 1 \\ \end{bmatrix}^{-1}$

The vanishing point is at the center of the image if the road plane is flat and perpendicular to an image plane. $(x_v, y_v) = (\frac{w}{2}, \frac{h}{2})$ (Page 7)(Page 30):

Horizontal Vanishing Point (roll $\phi=0$, pitch $\theta=0$, yaw $\psi \neq 0$):

$\begin{aligned} vp_h^*(\phi, \theta, \psi) &= [cos(\phi)sin(\psi)-sin(\phi)sin(\theta)cos(\psi), -(sin(\phi)sin(\psi)+cos(\phi)sin(\theta)cos(\psi)), cos(\theta)cos(\psi)]^T\\ &= [cos(0)sin(\psi)-sin(0)sin(0)cos(\psi), -(sin(0)sin(\psi)+cos(0)sin(0)cos(\psi)), cos(0)cos(\psi)]^T\\ &= [sin(\psi), 0, cos(\psi)]^T\\ &= [tan(\psi), 0, 1]^T \end{aligned}$


$\begin{aligned}O_{image}^{'} &= M^{'}K[R|t] O_{world}\\ &= \begin{bmatrix} -1 & 0 & 2x_v\\ 0 & -1 & 2y_v\\ 0 & 0 & 1\\ \end{bmatrix} K\ [R|t] \\ &= \begin{bmatrix} -1 & 0 & 2x_v\\ 0 & -1 & 2y_v\\ 0 & 0 & 1\\ \end{bmatrix} \begin{bmatrix} f & 0 & \frac{w}{2}\\ 0 & f & \frac{h}{2} \\ 0 & 0 & 1 \end{bmatrix} [R|t] \\ &= \begin{bmatrix} -f & 0 & 2x_v-\frac{w}{2}\\ 0 & -f & 2y_v-\frac{h}{2} \\ 0 & 0 & 1 \end{bmatrix} [R|t] \\ &= K^{'}\ [R|t]\end{aligned}$

$\begin{aligned}O_{image}^{'} &= KM^{'}[R|t] O_{world}\\ &= K\begin{bmatrix} -1 & 0 & 0\\ 0 & -1 & 0\\ 0 & 0 & 1\\ \end{bmatrix}\ [R|t] \\ &= \begin{bmatrix} f & 0 & \frac{w}{2}\\ 0 & f & \frac{h}{2} \\ 0 & 0 & 1 \end{bmatrix} \begin{bmatrix} -1 & 0 & 0\\ 0 & -1 & 0\\ 0 & 0 & 1\\ \end{bmatrix} [R|t] \\ &= \begin{bmatrix} -f & 0 & \frac{w}{2}\\ 0 & -f & \frac{h}{2} \\ 0 & 0 & 1 \end{bmatrix} [R|t] \\ &= K^{'}\ [R|t]\end{aligned}$
